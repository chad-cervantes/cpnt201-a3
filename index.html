About

Our Story

Sutāai Knife Co. is a Japanese knife company from Osaka, Japan. Established in 1995, we strive our best to produce and give the highest quality standard of knives. With 10 locations now across South Korea, the Philippines, and China, we are proud to announce that we will be setting up shops in New Zealand and Australia!

The founders, the Sukiyaki family, has been making knives for four generations. Starting in 1930, in their small shop in Osaka, the tradition of Japanese knife making has been passed down from generation to generation. Today, the grand-children of the founder, Saiba Sukiyaki, are the ones who lead the company towards being the best of the best.

At Sutāai, we believe that making Japanese knives shouldn't be too expensive for people to buy So our vision is that we will strongly ensure and commit, to you, that our knives meet the highest quality standards, at an affordable price for everyone.
